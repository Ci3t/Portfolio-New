## Visual Direction

- Use a vertical or diagonal timeline.
- Each role appears as a "chronicle record."
- Left side: date and company.
- Right side: impact bullets and stack/proof chips.
- Add a small "signal gained" label per role:
  - Appleseeds: Teaching / Leadership
  - Samplead: Cloud Events / Automation
  - Strauss: Troubleshooting / Support

## Animation Ideas

- Timeline line draws as user scrolls.
- Role cards reveal with hard horizontal masks.
- Active role locks into focus while previous roles dim.
- Impact numbers can count up: `25+ students`, `720-hour bootcamp` in education.

## HR/Dev Clarity

Keep company, role, and dates easy to scan. Recruiters should not need to decode section labels.
